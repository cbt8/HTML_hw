Weather and Latitude

Humans have settled overwhelmingly in the Northern Hemisphere. About 88 percent of the world's population lives north of the equator, and about 68 percent of the world's landmass is likewise in the northern hemisphere. Latitude makes a big difference for the experience of humans living a certain distance from the equator. How does latitude affect weather, on average? This project seeks to analyze weather patterns in human cities considered in terms of latitude. We consider four variables: maximum temperature, humidity, cloudiness, and wind speed.
